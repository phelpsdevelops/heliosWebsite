How It Works

Our MVP is a 10×10 portable solar panel engineered for rugged use and simple deployment. It’s compact, lightweight, and requires no technical setup — just unfold and use. The panel connects to basic devices through USB-C or small DC output and can integrate with low-power water testing tools or portable purification systems.


Logistics, the panel is designed for modular shipping so municipalities and relief agencies can order in bulk. Its durability allows deployment in disaster zones, rural areas, and regions without grid access. The simplicity of the design minimizes maintenance, making it ideal for communities with limited technical capacity.”
